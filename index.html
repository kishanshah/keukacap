Services

Keuka Capital LLC (“KeukaCap”) provides affordable, accountable, and transparent investment advice to individual investors.

Personal Investment Plan

KeukaCap establishes a personal investment plan for your account, based on your goals, finances, and lifestyle.

Managed Accounts

KeukaCap constructs an investment portfolio for you and provides ongoing portfolio management. We help you open your brokerage account, identify an appropriate asset allocation, and recommend specific stocks, bonds, and exchange traded funds (“ETF”) investments for your account.

Total Return Fund

KeukaCap is incubating an investment fund focused on maximizing long-term returns that will be available to select clients.

Investment Approach

Create Your Personalized Investment Plan.

Accountability

KeukaCap establishes your investment plan, helps you open a brokerage account, and constructs your investment portfolio. We recommend an asset allocation and execute that allocation by investing directly in individual stocks, bonds, and ETF's. This allows clients to hold a more concentrated portfolio of under-valued securities and makes executing your investment plan more efficient. We are accountable to our clients for our investment plans, asset allocation decisions, and security recommendations.

Transparency

All investments are made within your personal brokerage account. This eliminates the costs associated with large fund bureaucracies and provides you immediate access to and control of your money. You also benefit from a clearer understanding of what companies you are investing in, a level of transparency that rarely exists with large funds.

Affordability

KeukaCap charges annual fees equal to 0.5% of money under management. So for every $10,000 managed by KeukaCap, we are paid $50 annually. Most investment advisors charge 1.0%-2.0% annually, and some have upfront fees as high as 5.0%. Paying lower fees leads to significantly higher investment returns, as fee savings are reinvested and compound over time.

Expertise

Few investment advisors are trained to both construct and execute an investment plan. KeukaCap's founder is a Chartered Financial Analyst (CFA), a member of the New York Society of Security Analysts (NYSSA), a former sell-side equity research analyst, and has experience in both consulting and corporate finance. He has been managing money for over ten years.

Responsibility

KeukaCap embraces the highest ethical standards in all business endeavours. We value our brand name and reputation over all else. Your best interests are our priority and our primary objective is to achieve your investment goals, as laid out in your personal investment plan.

Long-Term

KeukaCap does not engage in day-trading or derivative investing. Our investment approach dictates that we focus on the long-term prospects of a business and identify undervalued companies that will appreciate considerably over the long-term. We favor diversified, conservative portfolios that typically outperform during bear markets and underperform during bull markets.

About

KeukaCap was formed in response to a problem: individuals with excess capital to invest have few affordable, transparent, and accountable options.

  • 2007-2009

    An Investor is Born

    KeukaCap’s founder begins managing personal and family money while working in consulting full-time. Markets are nearing all-time highs. Due to his value-oriented approach, few stocks make the cut. The following 2008-2009 downturn punishes many investors. He counsels family investors through the downturn, urging them to hang tight or add to their stock positions.

  • 2010 - 2012

    Valuable Training

    KeukaCap’s founder gains valuable fundamental equity analysis experience while researching aerospace and defense stocks under a former Northrop Grumman aerospace engineer/Goldman Sachs lead analyst. But the sell-side’s short term focus disagrees with him. He continues managing his own money, with a long-term, value orientation.

  • 2013 - 2014

    The Makings of KeukaCap

    KeukaCap’s founder begins managing family money while gaining valuable corporate finance and operations experience at Ports America. The seeds of Keuka Capital are planted. Keuka Capital begins incubating a total return investment fund with the goal of outperforming the S&P 500 over five to ten year periods.

  • 2015

    Launch

    KeukaCap launches its separately managed account service for individual investor clients.

Founder Profile

Reuben Dvoretsky, CFA

Founder and Portfolio Manager

Reuben is the Founder and Portfolio Manager at Keuka Capital LLC. He is currently employed full-time as a Senior Manager at Ports America Group, the country’s largest shipping terminal operator. At Ports America, Reuben manages financial projects, participates in acquisition due diligence, and builds financial models.

Prior to joining Ports America, Reuben worked as an Associate Analyst at the Buckingham Research Group, where he researched and issued investment recommendations on Aerospace & Defense stocks. Reuben was responsible for financial modeling, creating industry analyses, generating investment ideas, and communicating findings and recommendations to clients. Reuben started his career as a consultant with Huron Consulting Group in New York, where he was a member of the financial consulting practice. Reuben’s clients included major law and private equity firms, for whom he provided valuation and analytical services.

Reuben is a Chartered Financial Analyst (CFA) and a member of the New York Society of Security Analysts (NYSSA).

Reuben holds a BBA from the Ross School of Business at the University of Michigan with emphases in finance, accounting, and corporate strategy.
